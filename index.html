About Me

نبذة عني

With over twelve years of experience as an electrical engineer, I have developed extensive expertise in project management, infrastructure development, and electrical systems design. My career has spanned multiple countries and challenging projects, including work in Saudi Arabia's holy sites and major industrial installations.

My professional approach combines technical excellence with effective leadership, allowing me to successfully manage teams of up to 150 electricians and 300 workers during critical projects. I specialize in designing, installing, testing, and maintaining electrical systems for diverse applications, from hospital facilities to pilgrim accommodations.

I am continuously enhancing my skillset through professional development courses in renewable energy, project management, and emerging technologies like AI. My goal is to deliver electrical engineering solutions that are innovative, reliable, and sustainable.
